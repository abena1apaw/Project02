Stress is an unavoidable fact of life. Physiology of stress is evolutionary development to help us adapt to danger. Now stress is triggered over any change or unfamiliar situation. Handling stress is the key to living a meaningful and properous life.

Ten Minute Meditation

  1. Surround yourself in a peaceful setting
  2. Sit in a seat, on the ground, or lay down with your back to the floor.
  3. Let go of any anxious thoughts
  4. Focus your thoughts on a space on the wall or ceiling
  5. think only about your breath
  6. Take the time to feel any part of your body that is tense
  7. Wiggle your toes and hands
  8. Stretch out your limbs, may be do some yoga stretches
